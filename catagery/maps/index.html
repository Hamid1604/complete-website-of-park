Facilities

Enjoy your visit to Bearizona Wildlife Park with our convenient facilities:

Visitor Center

Start your journey at our well-equipped visitor center with information about the park, maps, and friendly staff to assist you.

Rest Areas

Take a break and relax in our designated rest areas, strategically placed throughout the park for your convenience.

Snack Bar

Refuel with a variety of snacks and beverages available at our snack bar. Enjoy a quick bite while surrounded by nature.

Souvenir Shop

Commemorate your visit with unique souvenirs available at our on-site shop. Find gifts for friends, family, or yourself.

Picnic Areas

Pack a picnic and make use of our designated picnic areas. Enjoy your food surrounded by the picturesque scenery of the park.

Restrooms

Convenient restroom facilities are available throughout the park for your comfort and convenience.

National parks preserve and protect ecologically significant areas, fostering biodiversity and providing opportunities for outdoor recreation.
